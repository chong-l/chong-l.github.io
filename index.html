Biography

I'm a Data Science Institute Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Chicago hosted by Prof. Yuxin Chen and Prof. Rebecca Willett. Starting Fall 2024, I'll join the University at Albany, State University of New York as an Assistant Professor of Computer Science. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UC Santa Barbara, advised by Prof. Yu-Xiang Wang. I'm an IEEE-TNNLS associate editor, JMLR editorial board reviewer, and program committee member of ICML, NeurIPS, ICLR, AISTATS, ISMB, AAAI, KDD, SDM conferences and ICML workshop proposals. I'm also the lead organizer of the NeurIPS 2023 Workshop on New Frontiers of AI for Drug Discovery and Development (AI4D3-2023).

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning: Bayesian optimization, bandit algorithms, active learning.
  • AI for Science: experimental design, drug discovery, and materials science.
